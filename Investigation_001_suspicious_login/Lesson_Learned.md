# Lessons Learned

## What I Learned

- Multiple failed logins followed by a successful login may indicate account compromise.
- Evidence must always be collected before making conclusions.
- Not every alert is a confirmed incident.
- Proper documentation is essential in every investigation.
- Escalation is necessary when the investigation exceeds Tier 1 responsibilities.

## Skills Improved

- Alert Analysis
- Investigation Documentation
- Critical Thinking
- SOC Workflow
- Incident Reporting
