# Lessons Learned

## What I Learned

- Administrator account creation should always be verified.
- Windows Event IDs provide valuable evidence during investigations.
- Activities occurring outside normal business hours deserve additional attention.
- Privilege escalation is a common attacker objective after gaining access to a system.
- Proper documentation helps ensure investigations are consistent and repeatable.
